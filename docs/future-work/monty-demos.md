In this section we list items related to demoing Monty's capabilities on real-world applications. While we are mostly focused on developing a general purpose algorithm that isn't optimized for any particular application, we like to get insights into how well Monty actually does outside our test environments. 

Please feel free to share on our [Forum](https://forum.thousandbrains.org/) or [Project Showcase](../community/project-showcase.md) page if you have tested Monty in a new setting. We are especially interested in hearing about any challenges or limitations you encounter.
